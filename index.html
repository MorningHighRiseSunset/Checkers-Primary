Instructions

Follow these steps to play Checkers:

  1. Black moves first, followed by White. Players alternate turns.
  2. Pieces move diagonally forward to an adjacent unoccupied dark square.
  3. If an opponent's piece is on a diagonally adjacent square and the square immediately beyond it is vacant, you must capture the opponent's piece by jumping over it.
  4. Captured pieces are removed from the board.
  5. If a piece reaches the farthest row from the player, it is crowned and becomes a "king." Kings can move diagonally forward and backward.
  6. The objective is to capture all of the opponent's pieces or block them so they cannot move.
  7. The game ends when one player captures all the opponent's pieces or blocks them from making any legal moves.
